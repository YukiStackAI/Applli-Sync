Platform Capabilities

Everything You Need to
Manage Your Job Search

From automatic tracking to deep analytics — AppliSync gives you full visibility into your career pipeline.

Real-Time Sync

Automatically captures job applications from LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, and more via a lightweight Chrome extension.

Pipeline Analytics

Interactive charts break down your funnel — applied, waiting, ongoing, accepted, and rejected — all at a glance.

Smart Filters

Filter by role, platform, status, location, date, and salary. Instantly drill into the data that matters to you.

Status Tracking

Update application statuses in real-time. Track which companies have responded and which are still pending.

Cloud-First Storage

All your data lives securely in Firebase Cloud. Access your dashboard from any device, anywhere, anytime.

Dark Mode

A premium dark interface that's easy on the eyes during those late-night job search sessions.

Getting Started

Up and Running in 3 Steps

No complex setup. No learning curve. Just results.

01

Create Your Account

Sign up with Email, Google, or GitHub in under 30 seconds. No credit card required to get started.

02

Install the Extension

Add the AppliSync Chrome extension. It automatically detects when you apply to jobs and captures the data.

03

Track & Optimize

Open your Intelligence Command dashboard to view analytics, filter data, and optimize your strategy.
